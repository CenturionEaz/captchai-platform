# 📋 Acceptable Use Policy

**CaptchaIQ Platform — Acceptable Use Policy v1.0**

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines the boundaries of permitted use of the CaptchaIQ Platform. All users must read and comply with this policy before using the software.

---

## ✅ Permitted Uses

### Educational & Learning
- Studying AI and machine learning techniques through hands-on experimentation
- Learning about OCR, computer vision, and audio processing technologies
- Academic coursework and self-directed learning projects
- Building understanding of CAPTCHA security mechanisms and their limitations
- Portfolio development and technical skill demonstration

### Authorized Security Research
- Penetration testing on systems you personally own
- Authorized bug bounty research within defined program scope
- Academic security research with institutional ethics approval
- CAPTCHA robustness benchmarking on your own implementations
- Adversarial ML research in isolated, controlled environments

### Accessibility Research
- Studying CAPTCHA accessibility barriers for users with disabilities
- Developing alternative authentication mechanism research
- Analyzing inclusive design patterns for web security challenges

### Development & Testing
- Testing and debugging in personal development environments
- Building and validating CAPTCHA implementation on your own services
- Integration testing of security workflows in controlled sandboxes

---

## ❌ Prohibited Uses

### Unauthorized System Access
- Bypassing CAPTCHA on any third-party service without explicit authorization
- Accessing accounts, systems, or data without the owner's permission
- Circumventing security measures to gain unauthorized access

### Malicious Automation
- Automated spam campaigns
- Credential stuffing or password spraying attacks
- Scraping content in violation of robots.txt or Terms of Service
- DDoS augmentation or automated attack tooling

### Harmful Account Manipulation
- Mass fake account creation on any platform
- Automated account takeover attempts
- Bypassing age verification or identity verification systems

### Commercial Exploitation
- Reselling bypass capabilities as a commercial service
- Integrating into paid CAPTCHA-solving services (e.g., 2captcha competitors)
- Licensing or sublicensing this software for commercial anti-CAPTCHA purposes

### Privacy Violations
- Collecting personal data without consent
- Building profiles of individuals without authorization
- Using CAPTCHA research to enable surveillance or tracking

### Legal Violations
- Any activity violating the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)
- Any activity violating the Computer Misuse Act or equivalent national law
- GDPR/CCPA violations
- Violation of any other applicable local, national, or international law

---

## 🔒 Enforcement

### Self-Enforcement
Users are responsible for self-certifying compliance with this AUP before each use.

### Community Enforcement
The community is encouraged to report AUP violations via:
- GitHub Issues (for non-urgent concerns)
- GitHub Security Advisories (for security-related violations)
- Email contact (for serious violations requiring immediate attention)

### Consequences of Violation
AUP violations result in:
1. Immediate termination of license rights
2. Removal of contributions from the project (at maintainer discretion)
3. Reporting to relevant authorities if the violation constitutes illegal activity
4. Public documentation of egregious misuse (without revealing private identifying information)
